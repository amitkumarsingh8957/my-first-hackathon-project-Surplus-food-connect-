About Surplus Food Connect

Surplus Food Connect is a community-driven platform that connects surplus food with community partners. We believe that good food should never go to waste when it can nourish our communities.

Our mission is to create a sustainable food ecosystem where restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals can easily share their surplus food with local community partners and organizations.
